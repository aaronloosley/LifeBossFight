# LBF-054: Redundancy Meeting mission design and implementation

**Tag:** LBF-054  
**Category:** Mission Content

## Description
Create a work-crisis mission that helps users stay calm during or immediately after a redundancy conversation. This boss should focus on preserving information, rights, and next-step decisions under stress.

## Steps
1. Outline steps for note-taking, paperwork capture, severance questions, and follow-up scheduling.
2. Include a checklist of documents to collect and questions to ask before agreeing to anything.
3. Add clear disclaimers that employment law varies by country and role.
4. Test tone and pacing so the guidance feels calm rather than alarmist.
