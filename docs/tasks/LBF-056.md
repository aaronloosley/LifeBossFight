# LBF-056: School Complaint mission design and implementation

**Tag:** LBF-056  
**Category:** Mission Content

## Description
Add a mission to help caregivers structure a school complaint with dates, facts, and escalation records. This supports a repeat-use family category with strong referral potential.

## Steps
1. Map steps for incident logging, evidence gathering, policy lookup, and initial complaint drafting.
2. Add branching for safeguarding concerns, repeated incidents, and escalation to governors or district bodies.
3. Keep tone factual and child-focused rather than adversarial.
4. Test the mission with realistic school communication scenarios.
