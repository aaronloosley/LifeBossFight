# LBF-040: Privacy policy, terms, and consent requirements

**Tag:** LBF-040  
**Category:** Compliance

## Description
Create the legal and consent surface required for a real user-facing product. Even lightweight apps need clear disclosure about authentication, storage, analytics, and support processes.

## Steps
1. Draft or review privacy policy and terms of service.
2. Add consent language for analytics, emails, and optional uploads if required.
3. Ensure sign-up and settings surfaces link to the relevant policies.
4. Review final language with legal or policy stakeholders.
