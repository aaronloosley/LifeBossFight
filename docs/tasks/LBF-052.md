# LBF-052: Car Accident mission design and implementation

**Tag:** LBF-052  
**Category:** Mission Content

## Description
Create a guided mission for drivers or passengers dealing with a collision, from immediate safety checks through evidence capture and insurer follow-up. This boss is broad, high-stress, and highly shareable.

## Steps
1. Define the safest first-step flow for injury checks, emergency services, and scene safety.
2. Author branching steps for exchanging details, photographing damage, witnesses, and insurer reporting.
3. Add regional caution notes where legal obligations can differ.
4. Test the mission flow for speed, emotional clarity, and evidence-pack usefulness.
