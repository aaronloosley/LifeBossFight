## Mission design notes
Every new boss should preserve the same product promises as Burst Pipe:
- a calm first move
- evidence prompts at the right moment
- branching where the real world actually forks
- clear escalation guidance
- a useful summary or report pack at the end
- language that feels practical, not preachy
